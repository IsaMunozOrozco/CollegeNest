Research

The Research Process

By definition research is "the systematic investigation into and study of materials and sources in order to establish facts and reach new conclusions" (Oxford Languages). In other words, it is a rigiourous procedure for how we understand the world, whether from a micro (e.g., atomic, microbial, etc.) or macro (e.g., whole-body, societal) level. The National Institute of Health has a great article and inforgraphic, but we'll try and explain it furthur here.

The research procedure can generally be explained in a few steps (see inforgraphic below), but you must know that the process itself involves multiple steps and is an iterative process.
